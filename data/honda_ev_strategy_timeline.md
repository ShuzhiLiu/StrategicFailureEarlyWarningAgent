# Honda EV Strategy Timeline: 2024 - March 2026

## Research for Strategic Failure Early Warning System Backtesting

This document provides a detailed chronological timeline of Honda's EV strategy developments,
the ultimate strategic failure (March 2026 cancellation), and surrounding industry context.
All data is organized to support backtesting a system that should detect early warning signals
using only information available *before* the March 12, 2026 announcement.

---

## PHASE 1: AMBITIOUS BUILD-UP (January 2024 - December 2024)

### January 9, 2024 - Honda 0 Series World Premiere at CES 2024
- **Event:** Honda unveils the "Honda 0 Series" with two concept models (Saloon and Space-Hub)
  at CES 2024 in Las Vegas.
- **Key Details:**
  - Completely new EV series created from "zero" with "Thin, Light, and Wise" development philosophy.
  - Seven total models planned globally by 2030 (small to large sizes).
  - North America first launch planned for 2026.
  - Saloon positioned as flagship; production version promised to be "very similar" to concept.
  - Level 3 automated driving capability on expressways and regular roads.
  - 300+ miles range per model (EPA standard).
- **Source:** [Honda Global - CES 2024](https://global.honda/en/newsroom/news/2024/c240110eng.html)

### April 2024 - Honda Canada EV Investment Announced
- **Event:** Honda announces $15 billion (CAD) EV investment in Ontario, Canada.
- **Key Details:**
  - Battery plant, parts facilities, and vehicle assembly facility retooling.
  - Expected to create 1,000 new jobs; retain 4,200 existing jobs.
  - Up to 240,000 vehicles/year when fully operational in 2028.
  - Federal and Ontario government support totalling ~$5 billion (CAD).
  - Announced at event with PM Justin Trudeau and Ontario Premier Doug Ford.
- **Source:** [Global News - Honda Canada EV Investment](https://globalnews.ca/news/11177134/honda-canada-ev-investment-postponed-ontario/)

### May 16, 2024 - Honda 2024 Business Briefing (Peak Ambition)
- **Event:** CEO Toshihiro Mibe presents Honda's electrification and investment strategy.
- **Key Targets Announced:**
  - **10 trillion yen** total investment through FY2031 (10-year period):
    - 2 trillion yen: R&D for software-defined mobility
    - 2 trillion yen: EV value chain establishment
    - 6 trillion yen: Manufacturing/development
  - **40% of global auto sales** to be EVs/FCEVs by 2030
  - **2+ million EV units** production by 2030
  - **100% EV/FCEV** global sales by 2040
  - **100% EV** sales in China by 2035
  - **5% return on sales (ROS)** for EV business by 2030
  - **35% reduction** in production costs vs. mixed-flow lines
  - **20% reduction** in North American battery procurement costs by 2030
  - **12 trillion yen** operating cash flow target (FY2022-2026)
- **Production Plans:**
  - LG Energy Solution JV battery plant: production begins 2025 (40GWh capacity, $4.4B investment)
  - Dedicated EV plant in Canada: operational by 2028
  - 10 Honda-brand EV models in China by 2027
  - Honda 0 Saloon market launch: 2026 (North America first)
- **Source:** [Honda Global - 2024 Business Briefing](https://global.honda/en/newsroom/news/2024/c240516eng.html)

### October 8-9, 2024 - Honda 0 Tech Meeting 2024
- **Event:** Honda introduces next-generation technologies for Honda 0 Series.
- **Key Technical Details:**
  - e:Architecture platform details revealed
  - 2.0 GPa ultra-high strength steel for thin, low-height styling
  - Compact e-Axle with inverter downsized ~40%
  - Thin battery pack using Megacasting and 3D friction stir welding
  - Six 6,000-ton megacasting machines planned for Ohio facility
  - Battery degradation target: <10% capacity loss after 10 years
  - Active aerodynamic system
  - ASIMO OS vehicle operating system
  - IPU part count reduction from 60 to 5 via megacasting
- **Source:** [Honda Global - 0 Tech Meeting 2024](https://global.honda/en/newsroom/news/2024/c241009eng.html)

### November 2024 - US Presidential Election (Critical Policy Shift)
- **Event:** Donald Trump wins 2024 presidential election.
- **Immediate Implications:**
  - Plans to revoke Biden-era clean energy spending and EV mandates
  - Threats to roll back $7,500 federal EV tax credit (IRA)
  - Tariff threats against trading partners (25% on steel/aluminum, 25% on vehicle imports)
  - Signal to relax fuel economy standards
- **Source:** [CNBC - EV Tax Credit Risk](https://www.cnbc.com/2024/11/20/7500-ev-tax-credit-trump-republicans-likely-to-axe-ira-funding.html)

### Late 2024 - Honda Prologue Success (Paradox)
- **Event:** Honda Prologue (GM Ultium-based) becomes surprise EV success.
- **Key Data:**
  - 25,132 US sales through November 2024
  - Outsold every GM EV in 2024
  - Became America's 5th best-selling EV in Q3 2024 (12,644 units in Q3)
  - BUT: This was a GM-platform vehicle, not Honda's own e:Architecture
  - Note: Success of borrowed platform vs. untested proprietary platform = risk signal
- **Source:** [InsideEVs - Prologue Success](https://insideevs.com/news/737314/honda-prologue-sales-q3-2024/)

### December 18, 2024 - CES 2025 Teaser
- **Event:** Honda teases two 0 Series EV prototypes for CES 2025 unveil.
- **Source:** [Electrek - Honda 0 Series CES Teaser](https://electrek.co/2024/12/18/honda-0-series-ev-prototypes-unveilet-ces-hit-global-market-2026/)

### December 23, 2024 - Honda-Nissan Merger MOU
- **Event:** Honda and Nissan sign Memorandum of Understanding to explore merger by 2026.
- **Key Details:**
  - Goal: combine R&D, electrification, and autonomy efforts
  - Meant to compete with BYD and Tesla
  - Honda market value ~5x Nissan's
- **Source:** [Al Jazeera - Honda Nissan Merger](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/2/13/honda-nissan-end-merger-talks-scuttling-60bn-deal)

## PHASE 2: EARLY WARNING SIGNALS ACCUMULATE (January 2025 - May 2025)

### January 7-8, 2025 - Honda 0 Series Prototypes at CES 2025
- **Event:** World premiere of Honda 0 SUV and Honda 0 Saloon production prototypes at CES 2025.
- **Key Details:**
  - Honda 0 SUV: production scheduled for first half of 2026 in North America
  - Honda 0 Saloon: followed by global markets including Japan and Europe
  - ASIMO OS vehicle operating system announced
  - Level 3 automated driving expansion promised
  - Production at Ohio EV Hub confirmed
  - Acura RSX (performance EV) also planned
- **Assessment:** At this point Honda was still publicly doubling down on the 0 Series strategy,
  even as policy headwinds were already visible.
- **Source:** [Honda Global - CES 2025](https://global.honda/en/newsroom/news/2025/c250108aeng.html)

### January 20, 2025 - Trump Executive Order "Unleashing American Energy"
- **Event:** Trump signs executive order on inauguration day.
- **Key Provisions:**
  - Eliminate the "EV mandate"
  - Remove regulatory barriers to gasoline vehicle access
  - Terminate state emissions waivers limiting gasoline car sales
  - Signal intent to roll back IRA clean energy credits
- **EARLY WARNING SIGNAL:** Direct threat to the policy environment Honda's EV strategy depended on.
- **Source:** [White House - Unleashing American Energy](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/unleashing-american-energy/)

### February 13, 2025 - Honda-Nissan Merger Collapses
- **Event:** Honda and Nissan officially end merger talks.
- **Key Reasons:**
  - Governance disputes: Honda wanted Nissan as subsidiary; Nissan wanted equal partnership
  - Honda found Nissan's decision-making too slow
  - Honda viewed Nissan's Nov 2024 restructuring (9,000 job cuts, 20% capacity reduction) as insufficient
- **EARLY WARNING SIGNAL:** Failed merger = Honda must face EV transition challenges alone,
  without the scale benefits the partnership was supposed to provide.
- **Source:** [CNN - Honda Nissan Merger Ended](https://www.cnn.com/2025/02/13/cars/japan-nissan-honda-merger-talks-ended-hnk-intl/index.html)

### Early 2025 - Honda China Sales Collapse Accelerates
- **Key Data:**
  - Honda China sales fell 30.9% in 2024, dropping to 852,269 units
  - First time below 1 million in nearly a decade
  - Honda dropped 3 spots in brand rankings to 8th place
  - Foreign automakers ceded 33 percentage points of China market share since 2020
  - Local brands now ~69% of Chinese market (up from 36% five years prior)
  - BYD sold 3.83 million passenger cars in 2024 (all BEV/PHEV)
  - BYD commands 29% of China NEV market
- **EARLY WARNING SIGNAL:** Honda's competitive position deteriorating in the world's largest EV market.
- **Source:** [Automotive News - Honda Asia Challenges](https://www.autonews.com/honda/an-honda-asia-challenges-1110/)

### Q1 2025 - US EV Market Growth Stalls
- **Key Data:**
  - US EV sales growth essentially flat at ~1% in 2025
  - EV market share projected to reach only 11% by 2029 (policy roadblocks)
  - Production retooling for Tesla Model Y weighed on early-2025 sales
  - Hybrid vehicles absorbing demand growth while BEV adoption stalls
  - 25% tariff on vehicle imports announced (steel/aluminum also +25%)
- **EARLY WARNING SIGNAL:** The North American market Honda was building its 0 Series for was decelerating.
- **Source:** [Cox Automotive - Q4 2025 EV Sales](https://www.coxautoinc.com/insights/q4-2025-ev-sales-report-commentary/)

### May 13, 2025 - Honda Canada EV Project Postponed
- **Event:** Honda postpones $15B Ontario EV investment by approximately two years.
- **Key Details:**
  - Battery plant, vehicle assembly retooling, and parts facilities all paused
  - Cited softening EV demand and trade policy uncertainty (Trump tariffs)
  - Some CR-V production shifted from Canada to Ohio due to tariffs
  - This was the 2 trillion yen "EV value chain" investment from the 2024 plan
- **EARLY WARNING SIGNAL:** First concrete evidence Honda was pulling back from its own stated plans.
  Directly contradicts the 10 trillion yen investment commitment from May 2024.
- **Source:** [CBC News - Honda Canada EV Postponed](https://www.cbc.ca/news/business/honda-canada-ev-announcement-1.7533402)

### May 20, 2025 - Honda 2025 Business Briefing (The Pivot)
- **Event:** CEO Toshihiro Mibe announces strategic realignment.
- **Revised Targets:**
  - **EV sales target for 2030:** Reduced from 30% to "below 30%" (widely reported as ~20%)
  - **Investment:** Cut from 10 trillion yen to 7 trillion yen (3 trillion yen reduction)
  - **HEV pivot:** 2.2 million hybrid units targeted by 2030 (core powertrain during transition)
  - **Total sales target:** Above 3.6 million units
  - **13 next-gen HEV models** over four years starting 2027
  - **HEV cost reduction:** 50% vs. 2018 systems; 30% vs. 2023 systems
  - **Cash generation:** 12+ trillion yen over five years starting FY2027
- **Rationale:**
  - "Market slowdown due to regulatory changes and trade policy uncertainty"
  - Delayed dedicated EV production plants
  - Postponed Canada EV value chain project
- **EARLY WARNING SIGNAL:** Official downgrade of EV ambitions. The gap between the January 2025
  CES enthusiasm and this May 2025 retreat is a major red flag for the remaining 0 Series plans.
- **Source:** [Honda Global - 2025 Business Briefing](https://global.honda/en/newsroom/news/2025/c250520eng.html)

## PHASE 3: INDUSTRY-WIDE RETREAT (Mid-2025 - Early 2026)

### Mid-2025 - BYD Surpasses Tesla Globally
- **Event:** BYD surpasses Tesla as world's largest EV maker.
- **Key Data:**
  - BYD delivered ~2.26 million BEVs worldwide vs. Tesla's 1.64 million
  - China exported 2.65 million EVs in 2025 (doubled 2024 exports)
  - BYD overseas sales: 1.05 million vehicles in 2025
  - BYD targets 1.5-1.6 million overseas units in 2026
  - Ultra-fast "flash chargers" rolled out across Europe (400km in 5 minutes)
- **Source:** [TechResearchOnline - Tesla vs BYD](https://techresearchonline.com/blog/tesla-vs-byd-the-global-ev-race/)

### September 30, 2025 - US Federal EV Tax Credit Expires
- **Event:** The $7,500 federal clean vehicle tax credit (IRA) expires.
- **Key Details:**
  - Used EV credit ($4,000) eliminated at end of 2025
  - Only available for automakers that sold <200,000 EVs (reverting to pre-IRA structure)
  - EV charger tax credit set to expire June 30, 2026
  - Part of Trump's "One Big Beautiful Bill" tax reform package
- **Impact:** Removes key financial incentive for North American EV buyers.
- **Source:** [Kiplinger - EV Tax Credit Changes](https://www.kiplinger.com/taxes/whats-happening-with-the-ev-tax-credit)

### Q4 2025 - US EV Sales Collapse
- **Key Data:**
  - Q4 2025 sales collapsed following credit expiration
  - Full-year 2025 EV sales declined ~2% vs. 2024
  - Tesla held 46% of US EV market in 2025 (down from 49% in 2024)
  - GM at 13%, Ford at 7%
- **Source:** [Cox Automotive - Q4 2025 EV Report](https://www.coxautoinc.com/insights/q4-2025-ev-sales-report-commentary/)

### December 2025 - Trump Rolls Back CAFE Standards
- **Event:** Trump administration formally rolls back fuel economy standards.
- **Key Details:**
  - Proposed fleet-wide fuel economy: 34.5 mpg for 2031 (vs. Biden's 50.4 mpg)
  - Annual improvement: 0.5% (vs. Biden's 2%)
  - Eliminated fines for non-compliance
  - Eliminated EV credit trading between automakers
  - Reclassified crossovers as cars instead of light trucks
- **Impact:** Removed regulatory pressure that was driving automakers toward EVs.
- **Source:** [NPR - Fuel Economy Standards Rollback](https://www.npr.org/2025/12/03/nx-s1-5630389/trump-administration-rolls-back-fuel-economy-standards)

### December 15, 2025 - Ford $19.5B EV Writedown
- **Event:** Ford records $19.5 billion in special charges, pivots to hybrid/EREV strategy.
- **Key Detail:** F-150 Lightning transitions to extended-range EV with gas generator.
- **Source:** [CNBC - Ford EV Pullback](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/15/ford-ev-pullback.html)

### Late 2025 / Early 2026 - Big 3 Combined EV Losses: $52.1 Billion
- **GM:** $6.6 billion charge (but continues with Bolt at ~$35K)
- **Ford:** $19.5 billion writedown
- **Stellantis:** $26 billion charge; CEO admits "over-estimated the pace of the energy transition"
- **Source:** [Yahoo Finance - Big 3 EV Losses](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/2025-the-year-the-big-3-backed-away-from-evs-142434253.html)

## PHASE 4: THE FAILURE EVENT (March 2026)

### March 12, 2026 - Honda Cancels North American EV Models (THE EVENT)
- **Event:** Honda announces cancellation of three planned North American EV models and
  massive financial losses.
- **Cancelled Models:**
  1. Honda 0 SUV
  2. Honda 0 Saloon
  3. Acura RSX
- **Financial Impact (FY ending March 31, 2026):**
  - Operating expenses: **820 billion to 1.12 trillion yen**
  - Equity method investment losses: **110-150 billion yen**
  - Special losses (non-consolidated): **340-570 billion yen**
  - **Maximum total losses across fiscal years: 2.5 trillion yen (~$15.7 billion)**
- **Stated Reasons:**
  - "Starting production and sales of these three models in current business environment
    where the demand for EVs is declining significantly would likely result in further losses"
  - Declining EV market growth in the US
  - Unfavorable US tariff policy impacts
  - Reduced product competitiveness in Asia
  - Chinese market shift toward SDV and advanced ADAS
  - Inability to match newer EV manufacturers' pricing and technology
- **Revised Financial Forecasts (FY2026):**
  - Revenue: 21.1 trillion yen (down 2.7% YoY)
  - Operating profit: 550 billion yen (down 54.7% YoY)
- **Future Direction:**
  - Strengthen hybrid model lineup
  - Enhance cost competitiveness in India
  - Next-generation hybrid models for Asian markets
  - "Flexible" EV development monitoring market trends long-term
  - Establish appropriate fixed-cost structure
- **Stock Impact:** Honda stock at $24.64 (March 27, 2026) vs. all-time high of $34.94 (March 27, 2024).
  Approximately 29% decline from peak.
- **Source:** [Honda Global - March 2026 Announcement](https://global.honda/en/newsroom/news/2026/c260312eng.html)

### March 25, 2026 - Sony Honda Afeela Cancelled (Cascade Effect)
- **Event:** Sony Honda Mobility discontinues AFEELA 1 and second AFEELA model.
- **Key Details:**
  - Afeela was to be based on Honda's e:Architecture platform
  - Honda's cancellation of 0 Series removed the technical basis for Afeela
  - Afeela 1 sedan was already in pre-production phase at Ohio EV Hub
  - Second model (SUV) was planned for ~2028
- **Source:** [Sony Honda Mobility - Afeela Discontinuation](https://www.shm-afeela.com/en/news/2026-03-25/)
